On transmitter:

- mkfifo pipe.bin
- ffmpeg -f v4l2 -input_format mjpeg -s 160x120 -i /dev/video0 -r 1 -qscale:v 31 -f rtp rtp://127.0.0.1:9999
- socat -u udp4-listen:9999 open:pipe.bin,append
- ./ncurses_uhd_codec2_mod_file -i pipe.bin -a addr=192.168.10.2

On receiver:

- ./ncurses_uhd_codec2_demod_file -o data.bin -a addr=192.168.10.2
- ffplay data.bin

notes:
  wait for KB before staring play
